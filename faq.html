We get sent a lot of messages on a daily basis and we have the top 4 most frequently asked questions answered here. We hope this information helps in any way. And if not.. Buy a mag for you and a friend. :)

What does this magazine include?

HekMag has many interviews, pictures, quizes and news to offer to its amazing readers! We believe just by quickly going through the pages could get you motivated to skate or create art.

Do we ship worldwide?

We do indeed ship worldwide however for the time being we are currently having difficulty with shipping to the US and our team is working hard to eliminate any issues.

Where Can I Skate?

Anywhere they don't bust you, and sometimes even there. Most skating at the moment takes place in areas not intended for skating. People skate anywhere and everywhere throughout our cities. Over the years different types of purpose built skate facilities have been developed, but few have matched the challenge of the every-day surroundings of our urban lives.

Is there a way to participate in the HekMag weekly best trick competition?

Absolutely! All you have to do is post a trick on any social media platform e.g Instagram, TikTok, Twitter and make sure you include #hekmagweekly in the caption so we can review your post! Winner get a $100 and a free issue.
